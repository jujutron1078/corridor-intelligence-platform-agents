# Donor-Facing Proposal Structure Guide

## Technical Proposal Structure

### 1. Cover Page
- Project title, reference number, and client name
- Firm name, address, contact info, and submission date

### 2. Table of Contents
- With numbered sections and page references

### 3. List of Abbreviations and Acronyms

### 4. Executive Summary
- Concise overview of the understanding of the assignment, objectives, key outcomes, and approach
- A brief statement on why the firm is best qualified
- Often used by reviewers who skim dozens of submissions

### 5. Organizational Background and Experience
- Institutional profile and mandate
- Relevant past projects (3–5 short examples, each with client, scope, duration, and outcome)
- Demonstrates capability and past performance

### 6. Understanding of the Assignment
- Demonstrate comprehension of the context, problem framing, theory of change, structural realities, challenges, and goals etc, as expressed in the ToR. Include any other relevant sections as expressed in the ToR.
- Explain how the approach aligns with the donor's strategic priorities

### 7. Proposed Approach and Methodology
**The most important and heavily weighted section in all donor evaluations**

Should describe:
- Technical Approach
- Conceptual and analytical frameworks
- Research design or implementation strategy
- Stakeholder engagement plan
- Innovations and comparative advantages, etc.
- Include logical sequencing of activities and methods for validation or quality assurance

### 8. Work Plan and Deliverables
- Specify main tasks, associated sub-tasks, deliverables, milestones, and submission deadlines
- Gantt chart or table showing timing of tasks and responsible personnel

### 9. Team Composition and Personnel
- Organizational chart showing reporting lines
- Role descriptions and responsibilities
- Short bios or CV summaries (detailed CVs in Annex)

### 10. Quality Assurance, Risk Management, and Ethics
- QA system, validation process, peer review
- Key risks and mitigation strategies
- Ethical standards and compliance with laws or frameworks

### 11. Gender, Inclusion, Diversity, and Environmental Lens
- Explain how gender equality, social inclusion, diversity and environmental safeguards are integrated
- Required under OECD-DAC, UNDP, and FCDO evaluation criteria

### 12. Compliance Statement / Declaration
- Confirmation that all terms of reference are accepted and no deviations exist
- Optional inclusion of IP and confidentiality assurance

### 13. Annexes
- Detailed Work Schedule or Gantt Chart
- Key Personnel CVs (max 3 pages each)
- Company Experience / Past Project References
- Risk Register
- QA Framework
- Organizational Certificates/Registration

### 14. Bibliography/References
- APA Referencing Style

## Financial Proposal Structure

### 1. Cover Page
- Project title, reference number, and client name
- Firm name, address, contact info, and submission date
- Currency (must match ToR)

### 2. Summary of Costs Table

| Component | Cost Basis | Amount (Currency) |
|-----------|------------|-------------------|
| Direct Labor | Total person-days × rates | £XX,XXX |
| Other Direct Costs | Travel, logistics, data collection tools | £X,XXX |
| Overheads & Risks | % of A + B | £X,XXX |
| **Total Project Cost (without VAT)** | | **£XX,XXX** |

*VAT or withholding tax shown separately if required.*

### 3. Detailed Level of Effort (LoE) Table

| Role | Days | Rate (per day) | Subtotal |
|------|------|----------------|----------|
| Project Director | 5 | £1,000 | £5,000 |
| Project Manager | 21 | £300 | £6,300 |
| … | … | … | … |

*Include footnotes referencing deliverables to which each role contributes.*

### 4. Expense Breakdown (ODCs)
- Travel, communication, supplies, or software licenses
- Basis of calculation (unit rate × quantity × duration)

### 5. Overheads / Contingency
- Typically 5–15% of direct costs
- Must state what is covered (insurance, admin, risk buffer)

### 6. Notes and Assumptions
- Currency and exchange rate (if applicable)
- Payment schedule (e.g., 30% on inception, 40% on interim, 30% on final)
- Proposal validity (usually 90 days)
- Confirmation that cost includes all liabilities

### 7. Compliance Declaration
*The Total Price is inclusive of all direct and indirect costs, insurances, risks, and obligations associated with the performance of the Services. No additional charges will be claimed beyond this amount.*
